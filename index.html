// 01 — THE FORMAT

8 slots. First hack, first served.

No committee, no curation, no slides required. Show up, grab a slot at the door, plug in.

5m demo

Show the thing

Five minutes to demo your hack live. Bugs and all — especially the bugs.

5m Q&A

Then the room digs in

Five minutes of questions, heckles and "wait, how did you—?" from the audience.

8× slots

Dynamically allocated

Eight slots filled live on the night. Get there early if you want to present.

Slot allocation opens when the doors do — 19:00, last Tuesday(ish) of the month. Anything goes: code, hardware, a spreadsheet that does something cursed.
// 02 — MISSION BRIEFING

How to attend

Free, open, and friendly to first-timers. You don't have to present to come.

When
Last Tuesday(ish)
Monthly, more or less. Doors at 19:00. Check the next transmission up top for the exact date.
Where
c-base station
Rungestraße 20, 10179 Berlin. The legendary crashed space station on the Spree.
What to bring
A hack
Or just yourself. Side projects, experiments and noble failures all welcome. Laptop optional.
Cost
Free entry
Bring a few coins for Club-Mate and the bar. Donations to the station keep the lights on.
